# Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for **translator-bot**

Last Updated: 09/12/2025

This Privacy Policy explains how **translator-bot** (“the Bot”) collects, uses, and protects information when you interact with the Bot on Discord. By using the Bot, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 User-Provided Content

The Bot processes text that users voluntarily submit for translation.

Message content is processed only to provide translation functionality.

The Bot does not intentionally store translated text or original message content.

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Data

The Bot may collect limited technical data, including:

- Command usage (e.g., frequency, language pairs)
- Error logs and performance metrics
- Server and channel IDs (for functionality purposes)

This data does not include message content unless required temporarily for translation.

## 2. How We Use Information

Collected information is used solely to:

- Provide and improve translation services
- Maintain functionality and stability
- Diagnose bugs and prevent abuse

We do not use data for advertising or profiling.

## 3. Data Storage and Retention

Message content is processed in real time and discarded after translation.

Technical logs may be retained for a limited period for debugging and service improvement.

No long-term storage of user messages is performed unless explicitly stated.

## 4. Third-Party Services

The Bot may use third-party translation or hosting services to operate.

Text submitted for translation may be temporarily transmitted to these services.

These services process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party providers.

Examples may include cloud hosting providers or translation APIs.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent user data.

Data may only be shared:

- When required to operate the Bot (e.g., translation processing)
- If required by law or legal request
- To protect the rights, safety, or security of the Bot or its users

## 6. User Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- Request information about data processed by the Bot
- Request deletion of retained technical data
- Object to certain forms of data processing

To exercise these rights, contact the Bot owner using the information below.

## 7. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are taken to protect data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.
However, no system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for use by individuals under the minimum age required by Discord.
We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time.
Continued use of the Bot after changes are made constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

## 10. Contact Information

If you have questions, concerns, or data requests regarding this Privacy Policy, contact:

Open an issue in project's Github repository: <https://github.com/vitalegi/translator-bot/issues>
